What makes up a CSS box model?

A CSS box model consists of 4 different elements.these elements are:

  • Content
  • padding
  • border
  • margin

when we breakdown these elements we can understand what is going on.

  1. "content" is what will be displayed within youir box model.
  2. "padding" is the distance between your content and the border.
  3. "border" is a visible outline for your content and padding.
  4. "margin" is the distasnce between your border and other elements on your page.
