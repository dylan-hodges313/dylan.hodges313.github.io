What problem did you encounter and how did you solve it?

I ran into a fairly big problem with motivation. I started falling behind and felt like I wouldn’t be able to catch up and developed a defeatist attitude.

How did I solve this problem?

To begin I started by reaching out to my facilitator and talking to him about this. I even said I may leave the program. He told me he understood and gave me reassurances. This sparked a drive in me to catch up. I ended up talking to my Nana who is great with time management and keeping me motivated and asked her if i could spend a week with her and i can get everything done i needed to.

How did I feel throughout the process and what did I learn?

I felt motivated to catch up. I learned some very good time management strategies and how to prioritise my work.
