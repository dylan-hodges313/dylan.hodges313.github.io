How does understanding the principles of neuroplasticity benefit people?

To understand how the principles benefit people we first need to understand what the principles are. The principles that people looking to improve their learning experience need to understand are as follows: Use it to improve it. This is saying, the more you use that specific brain function the stronger the neuro paths will be. Repetition matters. Intensity matters. This is saying if you continue to ramp up the difficulty of the information you are learning you will see improvements in your learning capabilities. Time matters. This is saying changes occur at different times for each person. don’t compare yourself to others. In understanding these principles. You will stand to gain technically infinite knowledge provided you take the time to use and improve it and remember that repetition and intensity matter.
